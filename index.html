Conditions Appropriate for Referral

Nicole works with patients whose presentations are complex, overlapping, or underserved by conventional care — particularly those at the intersection of connective tissue, lymphatic, and autoimmune dysfunction.

Lymphedema & Lipedema

Primary and secondary lymphedema, lipedema stages I–III, lipo-lymphedema. Vodder-method MLD is the clinical standard of care.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Orthopedic, oncologic, reconstructive, and general surgery. MLD reduces post-op edema, supports scar tissue remodeling, and accelerates healing timelines.

EDS & Hypermobility

Hypermobile EDS (hEDS), hypermobility spectrum disorder, and related connective tissue presentations. Specialized approach for fragile connective tissue — standard deep tissue is contraindicated.

POTS & Dysautonomia

Autonomic dysfunction, orthostatic intolerance, and sympathetic dysregulation. MLD and gentle corrective exercise support venous/lymphatic return and nervous system regulation.

MCAS & Immune Sensitivity

Mast cell activation syndrome and heightened reactivity. Vodder-method pressure (<9 oz) and organic products minimize reaction risk; all session products are disclosed on request.

Autoimmune Conditions

Hashimoto's, lupus, Sjögren's, rheumatoid arthritis, and other autoimmune presentations with chronic inflammation, lymphatic involvement, or connective tissue overlap.

Chronic Pain & Fibromyalgia

Nervous system dysregulation, central sensitization, and chronic pain syndromes — particularly where standard massage has been poorly tolerated.

Postpartum Recovery

C-section and vaginal birth recovery, postpartum edema, and return-to-movement support. Programs are flexible and designed around each patient's recovery pace.

Movement & Neuromuscular Dysfunction

Faulty movement patterns, compensatory mechanics, and neuromuscular re-education — including assessment of visual and vestibular contributions to movement dysfunction.
